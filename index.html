SMSF History

SMSF历史

SMSFs have been part of Australia's superannuation system since the early 1980s. Today, they represent the largest segment of the superannuation industry by number of funds and total assets.

自管理养老基金自1980年代初就成为澳大利亚养老金体系的一部分。如今，按基金数量和总资产计算，它们代表了养老金行业中最大的部分。

1. What are the basic requirements to set up an SMSF?

1. 建立SMSF的基本要求是什么？

You must be an Australian resident, at least 18 years old, and have a clear understanding of your responsibilities as a trustee. The fund must have less than 5 members, all of whom are trustees.

您必须是澳大利亚居民，年满18岁，并清楚了解作为受托人的责任。基金必须少于5名成员，所有成员都必须是受托人。

ATO Resources 税务局资源

2. Can non-residents set up an SMSF?

2. 非居民可以建立SMSF吗？

Generally, non-residents cannot set up new SMSFs. However, if you become a non-resident after establishing an SMSF, you can continue maintaining it, but there may be tax implications.

通常，非居民不能建立新的SMSF。但是，如果您在建立SMSF后成为非居民，可以继续维护它，但可能会产生税务影响。

ATO International Tax 税务局国际税收

1. What are the benefits of having an SMSF?

1. 拥有SMSF有什么好处？

SMSFs offer greater control over investment decisions, potential tax advantages, flexibility in estate planning, and the ability to hold unique assets like property and collectibles within your super fund.

SMSF提供对投资决策的更大控制权、潜在的税收优势、遗产规划的灵活性，以及能够在养老基金中持有房产和收藏品等独特资产。

SMSF Association SMSF协会

1. What is the process to establish an SMSF?

1. 建立SMSF的流程是什么？

The process involves creating a trust deed, appointing trustees, registering with the ATO, obtaining an ABN and TFN, setting up a bank account, and creating an investment strategy.

流程包括创建信托契约、任命受托人、在税务局注册、获得ABN和TFN、设立银行账户以及制定投资策略。

ATO Guide 税务局指南

1. What are the ongoing compliance requirements?

1. 持续的合规要求是什么？

SMSF trustees must prepare financial statements, arrange an annual audit, lodge annual returns with the ATO, review and update the investment strategy, and keep comprehensive records for at least 5 years.

SMSF受托人必须准备财务报表、安排年度审计、向税务局提交年度报表、审查和更新投资策略，并保存至少5年的全面记录。

Advantages & Disadvantages

优点与缺点

Advantages

优点

1. Investment Control
1. 投资控制

You have full control over investment decisions and can choose from a wider range of assets including direct property, shares, and collectibles.

您完全控制投资决策，可以选择更广泛的资产，包括直接房产、股票和收藏品。

2. Tax Efficiency
2. 税收效率

SMSFs can be more tax-efficient through strategies like capital gains tax management and pension phase planning.

通过资本利得税管理和养老金阶段规划等策略，SMSF可以更节税。

Disadvantages

缺点

1. Time Commitment
1. 时间投入

Managing an SMSF requires significant time for research, record-keeping, and compliance activities. It's not suitable for those unwilling to devote time.

管理SMSF需要大量时间进行研究、记录保存和合规活动。不适合不愿意投入时间的人。

2. Compliance Risks
2. 合规风险

Trustees are personally responsible for compliance. Mistakes can lead to severe penalties, loss of tax benefits, or fund non-compliance.

受托人个人对合规负责。错误可能导致严厉处罚、税收优惠损失或基金不合规。

Hot Court Cases

热门法庭案例

1. Cooper v Commissioner of Taxation [2019] FCAFC 77

1. Cooper诉税务局委员案 [2019] FCAFC 77

This case clarified the definition of 'business activity' for SMSF purposes. The court ruled that regular and systematic activities aimed at profit constitute business activity, even if conducted on a small scale. This has implications for what SMSFs can legitimately invest in and how activities are classified for tax purposes.

该案澄清了SMSF目的下"商业活动"的定义。法院裁定，旨在盈利的定期和系统性活动构成商业活动，即使小规模进行。这对SMSF可以合法投资什么以及如何为税务目的对活动进行分类具有影响。

Case Details 案例详情

2. Aussiegolfa v Commissioner of Taxation [2018] FCAFC 122

2. Aussiegolfa诉税务局委员案 [2018] FCAFC 122

This case dealt with the in-house asset rules and related party transactions. The Full Federal Court provided important guidance on what constitutes an investment in a related trust and how the in-house asset rules apply to investments between an SMSF and related parties.

该案涉及内部资产规则和相关方交易。联邦法院全席法庭就什么构成对相关信托的投资以及内部资产规则如何适用于SMSF和相关方之间的投资提供了重要指导。
